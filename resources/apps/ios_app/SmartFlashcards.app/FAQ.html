1. How do I find Expert Decks for my course?

    1. On the Expert Decks tab, select “Browse more Expert Decks”. You can browse by category, or use the search bar. Once you get a new Expert Deck, you’ll find it in your Expert Decks tab.


  2. How do I study the cards inside Expert Decks and My Decks?

    1. Click on the deck you want to study, then click "Practice" to start studying.


  3. How do I create new flashcards from scratch?

    1. If you already created your own My Deck that you’d like to add cards to, click on the deck you want to add cards to. Select “View / Edit Cards”, and click the + sign in the top right. You can create new flashcards individually or auto-create flashcards from your notes by uploading .docx files stored in your Google Drive.
    2. If you don’t have an existing My Deck to add cards to, create a new deck first by selecting the + sign in the top right of your My Decks tab, enter the deck information, and then select Add Cards.


  4. How do I create a new deck with everything I need to know for my upcoming test?

    1. In the My Decks tab, select the + sign in the top right to create a new My Deck. In this, you can add flashcards you create individually or by uploading your class notes. You can also copy Expert Decks into your My Deck so that everything you need to study is inside one deck.


  5. How do I create flashcards from my class notes and other documents?

    1. Click on the deck you want to add cards to. Select “View / Edit Cards”, and click the + sign in the top right. You can auto-create flashcards from your notes by uploading .docx files stored in your Google Drive. (Upload your .docx files to Google Drive first)


  6. Why is my progress bar lower than it was last time I studied 4 days ago?

    1. Studies show you can forget information if you don’t keep practicing and studying it. For this reason, your progress bar decreases a little every day that you don’t study a deck. Keep practicing daily to keep your progress bar up (and not forget what you’ve studied.)


  7. How do I copy a deck to another deck?

    1. Select the 3 dots in the right corner of the deck you want to copy. Select “Copy To”, select the My Deck that you want to copy it to, and select “Copy Now” in the bottom right. Those cards will now be copied into that My Deck.


  8. How do I set notifications to remind me to study?

    1. You can set unique notifications for each deck. Select the 3 dots in the right corner of the deck you want reminders for, and click “Edit Deck Info”. Then, you can adjust the notification frequency and time. You must enter an exam date for that deck for notifications to be available.


  9. Can I use Pearson Prep on my computer?

    1. Soon! Pearson Prep will be available on web later this year and will sync with the mobile app.


  10. If I have a problem, what do I do?

    1. Click the Settings tab in the bottom toolbar, and click Support. This will open the Pearson Support site, where you can search Pearson Prep help guides and contact the local Pearson support team.
